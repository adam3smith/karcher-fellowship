About the fellowship

Spend two weeks in a historic Syracuse apartment, improving your mastery of one or more instruments of your choice.

The fellowship includes free room and board with organinc meals prepared by a personal chef, a weekly stipend of US$350.-, private moving screenings, as well as weekend excursions into the scenic finger lakes region of upstates new York.

The foundation will also support purchase of a new instrument with up to US$800.- (especially trombones.)

How to apply

How to apply

Send an informal description of your proposed course of study as well as proposed date to the foundation's chairman
